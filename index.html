Crystal Collector

How to play:

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Number to Get"
  2. There are four crystals below.
    1. Blue
    2. Green
    3. Red
    4. Yellow
  3. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  4. You win the game by matching your total score to the provided random number.
  5. You will lose the game if your total score goes above the random number
  6. The value that each crystal provides is hidden from you until you click on it, but will randomize between " "
  7. Each time the game starts, the game will randomly assign the values of each crystal.
